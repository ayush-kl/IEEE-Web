    Audience

    1st IEEE Sensors Council in association with TIH (Technology Innovation Hub) and ISSS (Institute of Smart Structure and System) is organizing “Winter School” which is scheduled to be held at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, Assam, India, on the 5th and 6th of December 2023. The IWS 2023 aims to offer a great opportunity to bring together professors, researchers, Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and scholars around the world with a platform to deliver the latest innovative research results and the most recent developments and trends in “Emerging nano/micro sensors and devices”. Please circulate this among your students. The winter school will feature invited talks from the president IEEE Sensors Council, distinguished lecture members, and eminent personalities all around the globe. The winter school will have workshops as well as poster presentations. IWS 2023 is technically co-sponsored by IEEE Sensors Council Inc., USA, TIH IIT Guwahati and ISSS India

    About

    The main objective of 2023 IEEE Sensors Council’s Seasonal (Winter) school is to provide exposure to researchers (especially from North Eastern part of India) working in the area of Emerging Nano Micro Sensors and related devices. There is no such activity in this domain and through the IEEE Sensors council, we are taking the initiative to provide a ground to budding researchers in this direction. This will give an opportunity to students to attend some eminent talks and help them choose a career in the future.
